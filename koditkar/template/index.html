About us

Kalewadi is a small Village/hamlet in Purandar Taluka in Pune District of Maharashtra State, India. It comes under Dive Panchayath. It belongs to Desh or Paschim Maharashtra region . It belongs to Pune Division . It is located 37 KM towards East from District head quarters Pune. 175 KM from State capital Mumbai

Speciality :

Mainly kalewadi come under rural region, in which Farming is main buisness of Villagers. In Kalewadi, High quality fruits like Custared apple, Fig (Anjir), Guava are get cultivated.

Towards Smartcity :

With time, Kalewadi is also moving towards becoming Smart Village. With the help of Social Leader, Village is adaopting new programs like - Smart Roads, Clean water, Beautiful temple etc.

Close Destinations :

Kalewadi Village is sourrounded by some good visiting places, like Malhar Fort, Jadhav Gadh Hotel, Khandoba Temple in Jejuri .

Our Projects

Our Projects Information

Clean Water

Clean Water is very important subject related to everyones Health. As it has direct impact of Human Body. So to remain Healthy, having Clean Water is need of current time. By understanding need of it, Villager have decided to set Big Water Purification plant for Village.

Water Harvesting

Because of Weather change, Water Harvesting is need of Human being. Farming is basic buisness of Village, for which Water harvesting becomes very Important activity to perform. Villagers are doing it with - e.g. Having Farm Dam, Storing Water in concrete Dam, Perculating water in land etc..

Smart Road

Swacha Bharat - Initiative from Govt. of India, is inspiration to have Smart Road in village. These Smart roads includes two lane roads with Concret foundation, roadside underground drainage, both side beautification with Trees, Fresh light with Led bulbs, Speed breakers, Magnetic Strips, Zebra crossing lines.

Beautiful Temple

Temple is place where everyone come together to have some spiritual programs, like - Kakda Arti, Bhajan, Kirtan. Temple is very Holy place, where every one can feel Peaceful, delighted Mind and get some Energy in Life. Everyone get inspiration and revelation also the Energy to cope with difficulties in life.

Happy Peoples

Being Happy is every individuals right in Village. Peoples in Kalewadi live very happly with positive attitude, spritual mindset, helping each other, participating in every program. Senior citizens always gather in temple to have some fun. On Diwali occasion, Villagers prepare some Drama and perform infront of all Peoples.

Organic Farming

Since ancient time, Farming is main business of Villagers. In current world, Organic Farming is taking much hype, which has direct impact on Human bodies. In Kalewadi, Organic Farming is practice from long time. With this, Farmers yields high quality fruits like, CustardApple, Guava, Fig (Anjir), Pomegrante.
