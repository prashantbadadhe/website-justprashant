Where It Began

Computers and Technology have become our life partners. The internet has become the integral part of people of present generation and has touched every aspect of our lives. My parents' occupation is farming and I have seen both of them toil day and night to earn a decent living for us. Every time I saw them work hard, I was overwhelmed with an intense desire to make their work less strenuous. I knew technology had the power and to acquire that knowledge, I embraced computer science. I developed a penchant for technology, which I further burnished over the years with zeal and fervor. The more I drowned myself in the ocean of technology, the more it dawned on me that this field has tremendous hidden potential and if used with discretion, can create heaven on earth.

That’s why My dream still stands tall and strong.

I wish to acquire a raised understanding of the nuances of the Computer Science field and use this knowledge to further develop innovations helpful for the common man. With a keen interest in working on the latest software technologies, I seek to take advantage of the plethora of opportunities in the field of data sciences which allows the tailoring of these technologies with one's creative instincts. Having acquired a solid foundation in the field of computer science through rigorous studies, along with my internships and projects in the IT industry, now I feel that it is the right time to solidify this base and expand my horizons in the Computer Science world.
